PROJECT

This project focuses on providing affordable and accessible internet and computer usage through Piso WiFi and Pisonet services. Located in Brgy. 24-A Balanok, Gingoog City, Misamis Oriental, the business aims to support the community by offering essential digital services for students, workers, and residents who need fast, low-cost connectivity and computer access.
